About Payday Cards

A passion project born in Austin, Texas, by two brothers with big dreams.

Our Story

Payday Cards was founded by Dallen and Devyn Larson, two brothers from Austin, Texas, who share a deep love for Pokémon and technology. With a vision to simplify the lives of Pokémon card collectors, they started this journey to create a tool that combines passion with practicality.

Though we’re still a small team with a growing user base, we’re committed to delivering a platform that collectors can trust and enjoy. Our dream is to turn Payday Cards into a community-driven hub for tracking, valuing, and celebrating Pokémon card collections.

Our Goals

We’ve built Payday Cards with three core principles:

  • Simplicity: To make card tracking and valuation as effortless as possible.
  • Transparency: To provide real-time, accurate insights based on current market trends.
  • Collaboration: To grow alongside our users, improving Payday Cards based on your feedback and needs.

These goals guide us in every update and feature we release, ensuring that Payday Cards evolves with the collector community.
